Biography

I am an MS student in Data Science at Worcester Polytechnic Institute, advised by Dr. Raha Moraffah. My research focuses on ensuring the safe development of AI systems, with current work on agentic misbehavior and scheming propensities as part of the LASR Labs fellowship in London. I am a Co‑Research Lead for AISST where I coordinate AI safety research for Harvard students interested in AI safety. Previously, I was a Data Scientist at Hologic, where I deployed machine learning systems to support the Quality Assurance, Supply Chain, and Data Analytics teams. I hold a BS in Data Science from WPI.
